Prawn and Coconut Soup

Description

A super quick version of this flavoursome Thai green curry with just 4 ingredients. Our simple prawn and coconut soup is ideal for a midweek meal.

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp Thai green curry paste
  • 400ml can coconut milk
  • 150g cooked king prawns
  • 250g courgetti

Method

  1. Heat 1 tsp flavourless oil in a frying pan over a medium heat
  2. Add the curry paste and cook for 1 min.
  3. Pour in the coconut milk, then leave to bubble away for a few mins before adding the prawns and courgetti.
  4. Cook for 1 min more to warm through, then divide between bowls.
